Tips & Facts

Simple actions can make a big difference. Here are some tips to help reduce your environmental footprint:

  • Segregate your waste properly and recycle.
  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • Plant trees and support reforestation projects.
  • Save energy by turning off unused appliances.
  • Educate others about the importance of the 5Rs.

Conclusion

Protecting the environment is everyone’s responsibility. By understanding global warming, practicing proper waste management, and following the 5Rs, we can make a real difference for future generations.
